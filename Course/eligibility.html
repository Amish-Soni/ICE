Description

  • What is the B.Tech.?

    Bachelor of Technology, a UG-level professional engineering degree course with a duration of 4 years. B Tech is a field related to computers and technology that includes a detailed study of programming and computer software applications. BTech courses provide the students with excellent theoretical knowledge and practical skill-based training. Since the B Tech course is more engineering and technology centric, the focus is on application and skill-based study, making industrial visits and internships an important aspect of the BTech courses.

  • Why study B.Tech. course?

    While there are a lot of specializations that extend bright future prospects for BTech graduates after pursuing B Tech to get into different careers, Computer Science Engineering is probably one of the most sought-after courses at present. This is also reflected in the high cut-off entrance exam marks that top engineering colleges including the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIITs), and the National Institutes of Technology (NITs) as well as some of the best central universities demand from the aspirants in order to enroll them for B Tech CSE course. All in all, the BTech degree promises decent earning opportunities for fresh graduates.
